Raised Letter Frames

Pricing & Info

  • Add $.10 (V) for White (subject to yellowing), Red, Blue, or Gray frames.
  • Add $.10 (V) for Side Panel imprint.
  • Add $.12 (V) for each additional imprint color (colors must have 1/16” space between them)
  • 15-18 working day production. F.O.B. Georgia factory.
  • Initial mold charge - $100 (V)
  • Art/proof charge - $35 (V) N/C if vector art suppplied.

Black polystyrene with White, Red, Blue, Yellow, Metallic Silver, Metallic Gold, or Standard Chrome foil hot stamp imprint. Add $.09 (V) for Premium Bright Chrome imprint which is specially formulated to provide longer lasting brilliance and maximum durability.

No art/mold charge when duplicating a competitor’s Raised Letter Frame (500 & up).

Digital printing requirements

Art must be sent at full size and must be a minimum of 150dpi. 300dpi is preferred for better quality. Vector art is also acceptable. A ¼ inch bleed is recommended for any art that extends to the edge of the plate. Acceptable formats are .jpg, .tif, .eps, .psd, .ai, .eps, .pdf, but all art contained in them must meet the requirements above.

For more information on this see our digital art specifications.
