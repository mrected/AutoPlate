Poly License Plates

AutoPlate’s unique Hi-Gloss Polyethylene License Plates are available in 4 thicknesses with Screen Printed spot color imprint. Plate size is 6" x 12”. Our exclusive .075” Premium Weight and .055” Standard Weight Hi-Gloss Poly license plates are for permanent use where a front state license plate is not required. Hi-Gloss .035” and .023” weights are primarily for temporary auto dealer use. .035” is suitable for reuse when a vehicle is sold. .023” is recommended for use in a frame for support. .035” is also suitable for promotional distribution for temporary use. Full bleed printing available at no extra charge. (Please add this to the Digitally Printed Poly License Plate page as well).

Spot color printing requirements

Art must be in a vector format. Vector format means your art was created in a vector program and saved in a file that saves the vector information, such as .ai, .pdf or .eps for example. Raster images, such jpeg, png, tiff, psd, etc, can be used but must be at least 200dpi, sent to the correct size, and can only be substituted for black images.

Art that does not meet these requirements may incur an art charge.

Placing a raster image in a vector file does not automatically convert the image to vector format.

For more information on this see our digital art specifications.
