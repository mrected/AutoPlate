Chrome Faced Frames

Black, Red or Blue polystyrene frames with the face hot stamped in Standard or Premium Chrome, Matte Silver (for the Cadillac Pinnacle Program) or Matte Gold. Pictured are black frames with recessed black copy in chrome panels or optional raised chrome copy in black rectangles that are recessed in the chrome panels. The sides and back remain the frame color.

Pricing & Info

  • Add $.27 (V) for our Premium Chrome we highly recommend for maximum durability.
  • Add $.43 (V) for Red, Royal Blue or Navy Blue frames.
  • Add $.18 (V) for Matte Silver & Matte Gold face frames.
  • Add $.18 (V) per panel for raised copy color in recessed rectangles other than face color.
  • 30-35 working day production time. F.O.B. Atlanta, GA.
  • Initial mold charge - $100 (V)
  • Art/proof charge - $35 (V) N/C if vector art supplied preferably on our template.

* NO MOLD CHARGE TO DUPLICATE AN EXISTING CHROME FACED FRAME WITH VECTORED ART AND A SAMPLE PHOTO PROVIDED. (500 & up)

Digital printing requirements

Art must be sent at full size and must be a minimum of 150dpi. 300dpi is preferred for better quality. Vector art is also acceptable. A ¼ inch bleed is recommended for any art that extends to the edge of the plate. Acceptable formats are .jpg, .tif, .eps, .psd, .ai, .eps, .pdf, but all art contained in them must meet the requirements above.

For more information on this see our digital art specifications.
